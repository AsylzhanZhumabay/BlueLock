What is the anime "Blue Lock" about?:

After the Japanese national team's failure at the 2018 FIFA World Cup, the country's football association recruits eccentric coach Jinpachi Ego, who believes that the only way to win is to abandon the outdated concept of team play. He opens a training boarding school called Blue Lock, where he recruits 300 of the most promising high school strikers from all over Japan. Of these athletes, 299 will be eliminated after a rigorous testing process, and the future of Japanese football will rest on the shoulders of just one player, the most productive and selfish. The story centers on timid striker Eiichi Isagi, who gradually rethinks his moral principles during this competitive struggle.
